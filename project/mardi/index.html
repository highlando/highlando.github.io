The one mathematics directed and driven consortium in the NFDI. Our institute has been a co-applicant and we are co-managing (together with WWU Münster) the part on Scientific Computing (Task Area 2 in MaRDI). My responsibility is the measure workflows in CSE where we are developing and implementing a description language for workflows in computational science and engineering with currently one postdoc working full-time on it.
